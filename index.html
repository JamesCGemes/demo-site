Athena Domain Inc.

Diversified Real Estate Investment and Development

Athena Domain, Inc. is a real estate development company that strives to create projects of high and timeless value. We are a diversified real estate company engaged in sourcing, developing, acquiring, disposing, partnering and managing of high quality real estate assets.

Areas of experience include:

Commercial & Mixed Use
  • Land Development
  • Retail and Restaurants
  • Medical
  • Office (Multi-story, multi-tenant and garden office)
  • Resort/Lake/Marina
Residential
  • Land Development
  • Class-A Multi-family
  • High-end Single-family Development
  • Master-planned Communities
  • Second Home /Rental

We only accept projects where we can embrace the already existing natural surroundings and work within the context of that natural beauty rather than fighting against it. We are also committed to the respect of our neighbors to maintain relational harmony to facilitate the creation and enjoyment of timeless master-planned residential, commercial and mixed-use developments.
